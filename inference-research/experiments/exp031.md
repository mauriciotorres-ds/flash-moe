# Experiment 031 — Lazy mmap weight loading (safetensors)

- **Category:** ssd_streaming
- **Applied on:** best config
- **Data source:** MEASURED (`measured=true`)
- **Decision:** **DISCARD**

## Hypothesis
Memory-map weights so pages load on demand from SSD.

## Rationale (bottleneck targeted)
Dense-model analogue of Flash-MoE SSD streaming; cuts load RAM.

## Prior expectation
Helps load/peak-RAM; Flash-MoE found mmap bad for COLD per-token expert access, but fine for resident dense weights.
